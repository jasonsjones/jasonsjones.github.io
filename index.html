Glad you found your way here

I've been a long time technology enthusiast and have wanted to start a blog for quite some time; however, I never felt I had anything to say that was worth the time to read. Also, being a tech enthusiast, I am just as intersted in the technology and platform as I am in the content, and up until now I had not found a blogging platform that inspired me to get in and get my hands dirty. That is...until recently. I've come across jekyll, a static site generator, and github pages--and now here I am with my very own blog.
