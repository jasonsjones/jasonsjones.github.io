Jason S. Jones — Blog

Glad you found your way here

I've been a long time technology enthusiast and have wanted to start a blog for quite some time; however, I never felt I had anything to say that was worth the time to read. Also, being a tech enthusiast, I am just as interested in the technology and platform as I am in the content, and up until now I had not found a blogging platform that inspired me to get in and get my hands dirty. That is...until recently. I've come across jekyll, a static site generator, and github pages--and now here I am with my very own blog.

One of the great things about jekyll is the number of freely available themes that you can fork and begin using right away to make a good looking right-out-of-the-box site. But where is the fun in that...? This site has been implemented from the ground up, relying only on a few third-party files, namely jquery and font-awesome. Yeah, I know you are probably saying you can tell it has been implemented from the ground up because of the apparent lack of design aesthetics. But I will say that beauty is in the eye of the beholder. I wholeheartedly acknowledge that I am NOT a designer, nor am I claiming to be one.

The intent of the project is two-fold. While the idea of starting a blog and having a platform to share my thoughts and challenges, and more importantly, share how I have managed to overcome those challenges (assuming I have) is a large motivator, I think the greater motivator at this stage of the game, is challenge of creating the blog itself. I have no doubt that I am going to learn a ton on this journey, and that excites me--so let's get to it!
